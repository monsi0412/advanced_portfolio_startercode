About Me

My name is Monserrat Diaz Martinez and I am a senior at All City Leadership Secondary School in Bushwick. I was introduced to web design by ScriptEd last year and have quickly developed my skills, helping my team come in 2nd at my first hackathon and 3rd at the second. I also attended the ScriptEd Summer Academy this past summer, learning bootstrap and designing a website for a client along with my partner. My interests/hobbies include listening to music, baking, and reading. I am also part of a sisterhood, Sister SAGE (Strengthening Advocacy for Girl's Empowerment) that does various community service projects.
